# Maison — Luxury Merchandising Analytics Dashboard

Maison is a synthetic luxury retail merchandising analytics dashboard built to demonstrate business intelligence, retail performance analysis, merchandising strategy, and forecasting workflows.

This project was created as a portfolio piece to explore how analytics can support luxury retail decision-making across product categories, regions, channels, customer segments, and collection types.

## Live Dashboard

[View the Streamlit dashboard](https://retail-merchandising-dashboard.streamlit.app/)

## Project Overview

Maison simulates a luxury retail merchandising environment using synthetic transaction-level data. The dashboard analyzes sales performance, sell-through, margin, inventory health, customer segments, visual merchandising impact, and demand trends.

The goal is to show how data can help merchandising and retail teams answer questions such as:

- Which regions and product categories are driving performance?
- Which collections show the strongest sell-through and margin?
- Where are stockout or overstock risks emerging?
- How do customer segments contribute to revenue?
- Which markets show the strongest future opportunity?

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Retail Overview
Provides a high-level business summary with KPI cards, revenue trends, regional performance, category performance, and key insights.

### 2. Merchandising & Collection Analytics
Focuses on collection performance, sell-through analysis, inventory health, visual merchandising impact, product opportunity scoring, and buying recommendations.

### 3. Advanced Analytics & Forecasting
Includes demand forecasting, customer segment analysis, market opportunity scoring, anomaly detection, correlation analysis, and strategic recommendations.
